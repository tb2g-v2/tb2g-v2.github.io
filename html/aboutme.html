About Me

Hi, I'm Andrew Avendano, but I usually go by Drew.

I'm a student in Aerospace Engineering and I love making my own silly little projects. Whether it's building robots, small voltage electronics, or just plain CAD, I'm all for it. I believe inventing and creativity should be as accessible as possible, so I'm a big advocate for using the internet as a space where people are encouraged to learn and experiment.

When I'm not breaking something, you'll probably find me running or nerding out about coffee... I swear, I can quit caffine whenever I want though, joking...ish

Most of what I build is self-taught, and I try to share what I learn so others can build cool stuff too. I believe if something doesn't exist, you should try to make it yourself, even if it's janky the first time around!
